How It Works

Setting up automated payments has never been easier. Follow these simple steps:

01

Connect Your Wallet

Connect your Cardano wallet (like Lace, Eternl, or Flint) to the application. We never have access to your private keys or funds.

02

Create Subscription

Enter recipient address, payment amount, and frequency. Give your subscription a name for easy tracking.

03

Fund Smart Contract

Deposit the required amount into the smart contract. You can fund multiple payment cycles at once.

04

Automated Payments

The smart contract automatically sends payments at the scheduled times. You can monitor or cancel anytime.
